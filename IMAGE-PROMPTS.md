## Image Generation Guidelines

### Technical Specifications
- **Hero images**: Minimum 2400x800px (21:9 aspect ratio)
- **Section images**: Minimum 1200x900px (4:3) or 1600x900px (16:9)
- **Portraits**: Minimum 400x400px (1:1)
- **Format**: WebP preferred, JPEG fallback
- **Quality**: High quality, optimized for web (aim for <500KB per hero, <200KB per section)

### Style Consistency
- Always include sacred gold (#C4963F) as accent lighting
- Deep forest green (#1A2E1A) should anchor darker areas
- Warm earth tones (#2D2419) for grounding elements
- Avoid harsh shadows; prefer soft, diffused natural light
- Include organic textures: bark, stone, earth, leaves
- Atmosphere should feel sacred but not religious
- Energy should be powerful but not aggressive

### Cultural Sensitivity
- Represent Bwiti tradition with respect, not exoticism
- Avoid stereotypical "tribal" imagery
- Focus on universal themes of transformation and healing
- When showing ceremonial elements, ensure accuracy and permission

### Mood Board Keywords
- Sacred, Ancient, Transformative
- Warrior strength, Compassionate guide
- Forest depth, Golden light
- From darkness to light
- Grounded, Authentic, Honest
- Safe container, Protected space
- Truth, Healing, Awakening
