In an era where depression, anxiety, addiction, and illness have become widespread challenges, this powerful medicine offers essential keys to restoring and maintaining wellness within ourselves and our communities.

Physical Healing

The Iboga root contains potent bitter compounds that rapidly cleanse the body and address numerous physical conditions including heavy metal toxicity, viral infections, skin disorders, Lyme disease, chronic fatigue, sleep disturbances, and digestive issues.

Spiritual & Mental Transformation

The visionary nature of this plant medicine often guides participants through profound inner journeys, enabling the release of past traumas and revealing the root causes of what prevents living with joy and purpose. Iboga effectively supports recovery from depression, anxiety, PTSD, bipolar disorder, attention disorders, addiction, and obsessive behaviors.

Addiction Recovery

Renowned for its remarkable anti-addictive properties, Iboga serves as a crucial ally in overcoming dependencies on stimulants, opiates, alcohol, nicotine, and various pharmaceuticals. Beyond physical detoxification and withdrawal relief, it invites deep introspection into the underlying reasons for substance use.
